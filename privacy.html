Privacy Policy

Welcome to ZiphyTech. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information when you engage with our website and services.

Information We Collect

When you register on our site, make a purchase, or subscribe to our newsletter, ZiphyTech collects various types of personal information, including your name, email address, and other relevant details. We also gather non-personal information such as browser type, IP address, and interactions with our website for analytical purposes.

How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used for several purposes, including:

  • Order Processing: To process and fulfill your orders.
  • Customer Support: To provide personalized support.
  • Product Improvement: To enhance our products and services.
  • Promotional Communications: To send you promotional emails and updates (you can opt-out at any time).

Third-Party Services

To improve our services and understand user behavior better, we may use third-party services. These services have their own privacy policies regarding the use of your information. Our website utilizes Google AdSense, which uses cookies to display ads based on your visits to our site and others. You can opt-out of personalized advertising via the Google Ads Settings page.

Our Advertising Partners

We encourage you to review the privacy policies of our advertising partners for details on how they utilize cookies and web beacons on our platform.

  • Google: Google Privacy Policy

Each partner operates under its own privacy policy, and we recommend visiting their links for specific details regarding user data.

Privacy Regulations

Our practices align with global privacy regulations, including:

  • GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation): If you're in the European Economic Area (EEA), you have rights to access, correct, or delete your personal information. Contact us at contact@ZiphyTech.com.ng to exercise these rights.
  • CPRA (California Privacy Rights Act): California residents can request information about their personal data collection, opt-out of its sale, and request deletion. Please reach out to us at contact@ZiphyTech.com.ng.

Security Measures

Protecting your personal information is paramount. We implement industry-standard security measures to prevent unauthorized access or disclosure of your data. However, please note that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure.

Your Choices

You have the right to review, update, or delete the personal information we hold about you. For inquiries regarding our Privacy Policy or to exercise these rights, contact us at contact@ZiphyTech.com.ng.

Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Changes will take effect immediately upon posting the updated policy on this page. We recommend checking this page regularly for the latest information on our privacy practices.
